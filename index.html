About Us

SaieTrix Solution Pvt. Ltd. delivers end-to-end IT services across hardware, networking, security and maintenance. We focus on practical, fast-to-deploy solutions with transparent pricing and reliable support.

  • Trusted partnerships with HP, Dell, Lenovo, Cisco, Ubiquiti, Hikvision.
  • On-site and remote support options with SLAs for business continuity.
  • Preventive maintenance & scheduled health checks for longer asset life.

Our Services

Comprehensive • Reliable • Local Support

Computer / Laptop Maintenance

Rapid diagnostics and repair for desktops and laptops — hardware troubleshooting, OS repair, driver updates and performance tuning. We restore systems while preserving data integrity and provide recommendations to avoid repeated failures.

  • Full hardware health-check (storage, memory, cooling) and component replacement quotes.
  • OS cleanup, driver rollbacks, and secure virus/malware removal with follow-up checks.
  • Advice on upgrades to extend lifecycle and improve performance (SSD, RAM, thermal paste).

Server Installation & Maintenance

Design, deploy and maintain physical and virtual server environments. We handle OS installation, RAID configuration, monitoring and backup integration. Focus is on uptime, security patches and predictable maintenance windows for minimal disruption.

  • Virtualization setup (VMWare, Hyper-V) and container guidance for efficient utilization.
  • Patch management, monitoring dashboards and automated alerting to prevent outages.
  • Backup strategy (onsite/offsite) including restore drills and RTO/RPO recommendations.

Printer Servicing & Repair

On-site servicing for inkjet and laser printers: jam resolution, print-head cleaning, alignment and mechanical repairs. We also support multi-function devices and print-server integration for office environments.

  • Consumables supply, scheduled preventive servicing and replacement planning.
  • Network printer setup, shared printing and access control for departments.
  • Service contracts with priority response for high-dependency printers.

CCTV Installation & Maintenance

Professional camera placement, wiring, NVR/DVR setup and remote-view configuration. We design systems for clear coverage and reliable recording. Our team ensures secure remote access and proper retention policies aligned with your needs.

  • Site survey, camera selection (PoE/IP/analog) and optimal placement for maximum visibility.
  • Network configuration, secure remote access and cloud backup options.
  • Regular firmware updates, health checks and storage management to avoid data loss.

Network Installation / LAN Setup

End-to-end LAN/WAN deployment: structured cabling, switch/router configuration, firewall basics and Wi-Fi planning for robust connectivity. We prioritize stable speeds, segmented networks and secure access for users and IoT devices.

  • Site survey with Wi-Fi heatmap, AP placement and bandwidth planning.
  • VLAN design, QoS configuration and firewall rules for segmented secure networks.
  • Documentation handover and basic training for in-house IT teams.

Attendance Machine Installation & Maintenance

Biometric and RFID device installation with seamless integration to payroll and HR software. We handle device calibration, network setup and data sync routines. Training and documentation are provided for smooth daily operation.

  • Device selection guidance (fingerprint, facial, RFID) based on scale and environment.
  • Integration with existing payroll systems and periodic synchronization checks.
  • On-site calibration, firmware updates and data export support.

Data Recovery Services

Fast, careful recovery for logical failures and basic physical issues on HDDs, SSDs, USB drives and memory cards. We diagnose, provide a clear estimate and attempt safe recovery pathways before recommending advanced lab-based recovery where needed.

  • Free initial diagnostics and transparent success-probability estimates.
  • Safe cloning, logical recovery and secure transfer of recovered data.
  • Emergency escalation options for business-critical data recovery.

Monitor / SMPS Repairing

Repairs for monitor display issues and SMPS faults — safety-first component-level repairs, voltage checks and thermal assessments. We advise on repair vs replacement based on cost, safety and expected lifetime.

  • Component-level diagnostics, capacitor/transformer checks and safe repairs.
  • Replacement quotes with OEM/compatible parts and warranty on repair work.
  • Post-repair testing and safety certification before return to service.
