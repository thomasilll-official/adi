About SaieTrix Solution Pvt. Ltd.

SaieTrix Solution Pvt. Ltd. is a leading provider of comprehensive IT services, specializing in hardware support, networking installation, CCTV installation, and annual maintenance contracts (AMC). We offer on-site repair, installation, and maintenance for laptops, desktops, servers, printers, time & attendance machines, and network systems. Our services are available across Maharashtra, with hardware supply extending nationwide. We proudly support major IT brands including HP, Dell, Lenovo, Asus, Acer, Canon, Epson, Cisco, D-Link, Ubiquiti, Hikvision, Seagate, and more. At SaieTrix, we are committed to delivering reliable, efficient, and cost-effective solutions to keep your business running smoothly.

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between technology and business needs, we have built a reputation for excellence through our expert team and customer-centric approach. Whether it's troubleshooting a network issue or setting up a complete CCTV system, we ensure minimal downtime and maximum satisfaction.
